The goal of the game is to guess today's plant!
Each day, a new plant is selected for you to guess. If you guess correctly, your win-streak will grow. However,
if you lose a game, your win-streak will reset.
Only the 49 plants that are available in regular gameplay are included.
Explode-o-nut, Giant Wall-nut, Sprout, and Reverse Repeater are excluded from the selection.
You have 6 guesses per day. If you spend all your guesses, you lose.

What does each seed packet mean?



- Plant: The first one is the plant you chose. It displays its name and image.

- World: The second one is the world of the plant. It's the area where you obtain the plant.

- Recharge: The third one is the plant's recharge time. It's the amount of time it takes for the plant to be fully
If the plant doesn't do damage, it will display "No" on the seed packet.

- Damage: The fifth one is the plant's damage. It's the amount of damage the plant does.
If the plant doesn't do damage, it will display "No" on the seed packet.

- Special: The sixth one is the plant's special ability. It's something the plant does that other plants cannot do.
If the plant doesn't have a special ability, it will display "No" on the seed packet.

- Upgrade: The seventh one indicates whether the plant is an upgrade of another plant.
If the plant is not an upgrade, it will display "No" on the seed packet.

- Sun: The plant's sun cost is the last piece of information to be shown. It appears on the left side of the the screen,
next to the plant's name and image.

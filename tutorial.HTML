Real tutorial



The goal of the game is to guess today's plant!
Each day, a new plant is selected for you to guess. If you guess correctly, your win-streak will grow. However,
if you lose a game, your win-streak will reset.
Only the 49 plants that are available in regular gameplay are included.
Explode-o-nut, Giant Wall-nut, Sprout, and Reverse Repeater are excluded from the selection.
You have 6 guesses per day. If you spend all your guesses, you lose.

What does each seed packet mean?



- Plant: The first one is the plant you choose, it's displayed the plant's name and their image.

- World: The second one is the world of the plant, it's the area that you get the plant.

- Recharge: The third one is the recharge of the plant, it's the time that the plant takes to be fully recharged

- Range: The fourth one is the Range attack of the plant, it's the area that the plant can attack or do something.
If the plant doesn't attack or do anything that involves area, it will display the seed packet "No".

- Damage: The fifth one is the Damage that the plant does, it's self-descriptive.
If the plant doesn't do damage, it's will display the seed packet "No".

- Special: The sixth one is the special of the plant, it's something that the plant do that others plants doesn't do.
if the plant doesn't have special, it's will display the seed packet "No".

- Upgrade: The last one is the upgrade, if the plant is a upgrade of another plant or not.
if the plant doesn't have special, it's will display the seed packet "No".

- Sun: The sun is the last information that's show up, it's shown on the left side of the plant's name and image.





DISCLAIMER: the images used in the seeds packet are figurative, it isn't mean that the plant does exactly what the image means
